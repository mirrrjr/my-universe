# Second Brain Roadmap
## Phase 1: Critical fixes
- Link every index to its local notes and every local note back to its index.
- Add related-note sections to reduce orphan notes.

## Phase 2: Hub creation
- Use the Second Brain Home and MOCs as the graph entry points.

## Phase 3: Knowledge consolidation
- Merge duplicate HTML reference/teaching explanations only after reviewing whether one is a lesson script and one is a durable reference.
- Split oversized roadmap/reference files into concept notes when they accumulate multiple reusable ideas.

## Phase 4: Long-term maintenance
- When creating a note, add at least one parent MOC/index link and two meaningful related links.
- Review orphan notes monthly and prefer wikilinks over broad tags.
